About Us

Passion for flight, powered by innovation.

01 / Our Story

The Beginning

The AeroModelling Club at IIT Ropar was founded with a simple but powerful idea — to give students a platform to turn their passion for flight into real, flyable machines. What started as a small group of enthusiasts has grown into one of the most active technical clubs at the institute.

From hand-built balsa gliders to GPS-guided autonomous UAVs, we have come a long way. Our members learn by doing — designing airframes, soldering electronics, programming flight controllers, and testing in the open field.

02 / What We Do

Our Activities

01

Aircraft Design & Fabrication

We design fixed-wing aircraft and multirotors from scratch, using materials like balsa wood, carbon fibre, EPP foam, and 3D-printed parts. Every aircraft goes through design reviews, stress analysis, and test flights.

02

Autonomous Systems & UAVs

Our autonomous drone team works with flight controllers like Pixhawk and PX4, integrating GPS navigation, computer vision, and custom mission planning software for fully autonomous operations.

03

National Competitions

We regularly participate in competitions like SAE Aero Design, Air Cargo Challenge, and various IIT tech fests. Our teams have consistently placed in the top ranks nationally.

04

Workshops & Training

We conduct hands-on workshops on RC systems, soldering, flight simulation, aerodynamics, and embedded systems — open to all students at IIT Ropar.
